Hide-and-Seek in the Flowers
Prompt

"I heard a Pokemon is playing a little game of hide-and-seek in the flower field. Let me know if you find it."

Target Pokemon

002

How to Complete

Research Camp

An Eevee Party
Prompt

"Eevee sometimes gets together for some playtime with the other Pokémon! Ever seen it do that?"

Info

Research Lv. 1

Target Pokemon

029

Eevee

How to Complete

  • Start the level near the camp entrance then take the alternate path inside the lab.

  • Upon entering the lab, immediately throw an Illumina Orb at the sleeping Stoutland and Eevee and then scan them to wake Eevee up.

  • Scan the TV to get three Cutiefly to emerge from behind it, and repeatedly throw Illumina Orbs at the leftmost Cutiefly until it reacts and flies over to Eevee.

  • Eevee and the three Cutiefly will head over next to Stoutland and start celebrating. Afterwards it will sit down and gently sway its head from side to side, which is when you take Eevee’s photo.

Sample Photo

"Oh, you were able to snap a shot! They're having so much fun. It makes me happy just looking at them!"

Florio Island Illumina Spot

Meganium's Pal
Prompt

"When Meganium gets to this spot, it starts looking around kinda restlessly. Do you think it's looking for one of its friends?"

Info

Research Lv. 2

Target Pokemon

028

Meganium

How to Complete

  • Follow Meganium as it walks through the level until it reaches the tree near the end. It will either walk in front of the tree or behind it. Keep restarting until Meganium decides to walk behind the tree.

  • Wait for Meganium to be on the far side of the tree, then throw fluffruit at the tree until a Hoothoot falls from the tree and startles Meganium.

  • Take Meganium's photo as it's startled and sitting on the ground.

  • Another method to get Meganium to go behind the tree more consistently is to throw Illumina Orbs at the second Crystalbloom as Meganium is sniffing it. This will cause Megnium to become excited and start running for the remainder of the level. It’ll do a lap around the tree which allows you to have Hoothoot startle it, but the timing on the fluffruit is tighter with this method.

Sample Photo

"Hey, they found each other! That's great! Looks like that sudden entrance came as a bit of a surprise, though."

Prompt

Info

Research Lv. 1

Target Pokemon

217

Snorlax

How to Complete

  • Take the alternate path to Snorlax’s lair by scanning the grass to your left after Dodrio has scared off Pichu and Scorbunny.
