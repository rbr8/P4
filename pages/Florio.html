Florio Nature Park (Day)

Hide-and-Seek in the Flowers
Prompt

"I heard a Pokémon is playing a little game of hide-and-seek in the flower field. Let me know if you find it!"

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

002

Pichu

Sample Photo

"Aww! What a cutie! Thanks for tracking it down!"

How to Complete

  1. In the flower field, throw an Illumina Orb at the clump of flowers nearby Scorbunny and Grookey (The same clump of flowers depicted in the prompt image).

  2. Pichu will pop out of the flowers. Take Pichu's photo as it's standing on top of the clump of flowers.

Where It Snacks and Snoozes
Prompt

Info

Research Lv. 2

Target Pokemon

012

Heracross

Sample Photo

"Good job finding it! Heracross seems very fond of this tree-- probably because it's full of delicious sap."

How to Complete

  1. Scan the dirt pile next to the first Crystalbloom to get Pinsir’s horns to stick out of the ground.

  2. Throw an Illumina Orb at the Crystalbloom or Pinsir to get it to jump out of the ground.

Florio Island Illumina Spot

Meganium's Pal
Prompt

"When Meganium gets to this spot, it starts looking around kinda restlessly. Do you think it's looking for one of its friends?"

Info

Research Lv. 2

Target Pokemon

028

Meganium

Sample Photo

"Hey, they found each other! That's great! Looks like that sudden entrance came as a bit of a surprise, though."

How to Complete

  1. Follow Meganium as it walks through the level until it reaches the tree at the end of the level. It will either walk in front of the tree or behind it. Keep restarting until Meganium decides to walk behind the tree (It seems that Meganium is most likely to go behind the tree if it spawns already awake on the right side of the starting area when the level starts).

  2. Wait for Meganium to be on the far side of the tree, then throw fluffruit at the tree until a Hoothoot falls from the tree and startles Meganium.

  3. Take a photo of Meganium as it’s startled and sitting on the ground.

  • Another method to get Meganium to go behind the tree more consistently is to throw Illumina Orbs at the second Crystalbloom as Meganium is sniffing it. This will cause Megnium to become excited and start running for the remainder of the level. It’ll do a lap around the tree which allows you to have Hoothoot startle it, but the timing on the fluffruit is tighter with this method.

Research Camp

An Eevee Party
Prompt

"Eevee sometimes gets together for some playtime with the other Pokémon! Ever seen it do that?"

Info

Research Lv. 1

Target Pokemon

029

Eevee

Sample Photo

"Oh, you were able to snap a shot! They're having so much fun. It makes me happy just looking at them!"

How to Complete

  1. Start the level near the camp entrance then take the alternate path inside the lab.

  2. Upon entering the lab, immediately throw an Illumina Orb at the sleeping Stoutland and Eevee and then scan them to get Eevee to wake up and walk around.

  3. Scan the TV to get three Cutiefly to fly out from behind it.

  4. Repeatedly throw Illumina Orbs at the leftmost Cutiefly until it reacts and flies over to Eevee.

  5. If done correctly, Eevee and the three Cutiefly will head over next to Stoutland and start celebrating. Afterwards it will sit down and gently sway its head from side to side, which is when you take Eevee’s photo.

Snorlax Dash
Prompt

"Snorlax usually seems to laze around, but you'd be amazed how it can move when it runs!"

Info

Research Lv. 1

Target Pokemon

217

Snorlax

Sample Photo

"I knew you could get the shot! That sure is surprising compared to its usual behavior!"

How to Complete

  1. Take the alternate path to Snorlax’s lair by scanning the grass to your left after Dodrio has scared off Pichu and Scorbunny.
