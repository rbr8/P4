Striking a Pose with its Tail
Prompt

"Skorupi has such a unique tail. See if you can get a photo of it striking an interesting pose with its tail!"

Info

Research Lv. 2 or higher

Target Pokemon

123

Skorupi

Sample Photo

"That's just what I wanted to see! It's always great when we can take a photo that shows Pokémon at their best!"

How to Complete

  1. Activate the three Crystalblooms around the sandstorm to get the sandstorm to disappear.

  2. Shortly after the sandstorm disappears, two Skorupi will meet up where the sandstorm used to be. Throw an Illumina Orb at both of the Skorupi.

  3. Take a photo of one or both of the Skorupi as they stand on their tails.

Kangaskhan's Parenting
Prompt

"Kangaskhan only feed their young if they feel the area is safe. You don't see it often, so keep your eyes peeled."

Info

Research Lv. 2

Target Pokemon

128

Kangaskhan

Sample Photo

"Hey, you were able to get a shot! Kangaskhan are very cautious by nature, so color me impressed. Good work!"

How to Complete

  1. Towards the beginning of the level near the three Crystalblooms, a Minior will crash near a Skorupi. Get the Minior to come out by throwing an Illumina Orb at the spot where it landed and is buried. Scan to help you find it.

  2. Take the alternate path to the left side of the oasis.

  3. The Minior will fly over to Kangaskhan, and then Kangaskhan will begin to play with its baby. When they finish playing, quickly play music.

  4. Throw a fluffruit towards Kangaskhan, but be careful not to accidentally hit it.

  5. Take a photo as Kangaskhan feeds its baby. Restart the level and try again if Kangaskhan itself ends up eating the fluffruit.

Rainbow Meteor Shower
Prompt

"We know that Minior cores come in seven different colors. I bet it'd be amazing if we could snap a photo of all seven at once."

Info

Research Lv. 2

Target Pokemon

130

Minior

Sample Photo

"Wow! That's quite the photo! Minior really are awe-inspiring... I guess I can't afford to let my skills slip yet!"

How to Complete

  • To complete this request you need to unearth multiple hidden Minior in the first section of the level by throwing Illumina Orbs at them.

  1. The first Minior will be to your right the instant the level begins. Scan the area to see exactly where it is.

  2. The second and third Minior are on the hills to your left. Scan the area to find where they’re buried.

  3. The fourth and fifth Minior will be near the big rock to your right. Scan the area to find where they’re buried.

  4. The sixth Minior will be to your left. Scan the area to see exactly where it is. You can actually see a portion of it poking out of the sand.

  5. The seventh and final Minior required can be found near the sleeping Silicobra. Scan the area to find where it’s buried.

  6. After passing over the hill to the Oasis, seven Minior, one of each color of the rainbow, will be seen flying in from the distance. Take a photo of this rainbow of Minior.

Prompt

Info

Target Pokemon

131

Silicobra

Sample Photo

"What?! Silicobra defeated Lycanroc? I didn't realize it was that strong!"

How to Complete

  1. Throw a fluffruit at the sleeping Silicobra to your right to wake it up and it will dig into the sand.

  1. At the start of the level, there will be two Sandshrew on a ledge to your left. Throw an Illumina Orb at the Sandshrew on the left.

  2. The Sandshrew will roll down the hill. There is a digging Diglett in its path. Hit Diglett with an Illumina Orb to get it out of the ground. Sandshrew will come to a halt after rolling into Diglett, and then dig into the ground.

  3. On the main path in the sandy area with the Sandshrew are more digging Diglett. Hit at least one Diglett with an Illumina Orb then take it’s photo or hit it with a fluffruit.

  4. The Sandshrew from before will reappear and both of the Sandshrew will roll down the ramp ahead. The second Sandshrew, with the help of Diglett, will roll all the way through the poison swamp and bump into the sleeping Scolipede, waking it up.

  5. Wake up the first sleeping Gliscor on your left by hitting it with a fluffruit.

  6. Eventually, the Gliscor and Scolipede will encounter and begin to fight. Take a photo of the fight with Scolipede as the focus.
