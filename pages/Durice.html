Shiver Snowfields (Day)

Frolicking Furret
Prompt

"I just saw the cutest Furret! I wish you could have seen it dancing, too!"

Info

Research Lv. 2 or higher

Target Pokemon

155

Furret

Sample Photo

"See? Isn't it just precious? Thanks for going to look!"

How to Complete

  1. Activate the Crystalbloom by the holes in the snow to get a Furret to jump out. It will run ahead and hide in another hole ahead on your left.

  2. Throw a fluffruit down that hole to get Furret to stick its upper body out of the hole.

  3. Play music then take a photo of Furret dancing.

How to Complete
