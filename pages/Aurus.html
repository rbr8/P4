Ruins of Remembrance

Houndoom's Breather
Prompt

"Houndoom always seems to be on guard, keeping an eye on its surroundings. I wonder if it ever gets tired."

Info

Research Lv. 2 or higher

Target Pokemon

201

Houndoom

Sample Photo

"Oh, I see! I suppose it stretches like this every now and then to release tension."

How to Complete

  1. Towards the beginning of the level, take a photo of the Houndoom on the left while it’s stretching. This is an idle animation that Houndoom will do at random, so restart the level and try again if Houndoom doesn’t do it.

Blowing Seeds
Prompt

"Have you ever seen Eldegoss heal other Pokémon? Just watch them closely."

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

202

Eldegoss

Sample Photo

"Ah, there you go! You see, Eldegoss's seeds are full of nutrients."

How to Complete

  1. At the beginning of the level, throw fluffruit at the temple until a Natu flies out and lands on the ground.

  2. Use fluffruit to lure Natu close to an Eldegoss.

  3. Hit the Natu with a fluffruit, then take a photo of Eldegoss blowing its seeds.

Just Perching Here
Prompt

"I threw a fluffruit earlier, and a Natu came down and perched on it! It was so adorable!"

Info

Research Lv. 1

Target Pokemon

203

Natu

Sample Photo

"Yeah, that's exactly what I saw! I got caught up in the cuteness of the moment, but next time I'll get a photo, too!"

How to Complete

  1. At the beginning of the level, throw fluffruit either at the temple or above the cliff to the left until a Natu flies out and lands on the ground.

  2. Throw Natu a fluffruit, then take its photo as it perches on top of the fluffruit instead of eating it.

A White Pokemon
Prompt

"Rita told me she saw a white, four-legged Pokémon around here. Let's get a photo of it eating fluffruit and check with her!"

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

204

Absol

Sample Photo

"I didn't know Absol lived here! That must be the Pokémon Rita saw, right?"

How to Complete

  1. At the beginning of the level, feed the Houndoom on the left a fluffruit. This will cause Absol to appear on the cliff above.

  2. Play music to get Absol to jump down from the cliff.

  3. Feed Absol a fluffruit then take a photo of it eating.

Salandit's Battle Plan
Prompt

"This is the aftermath of a battle! It was so exciting, I forgot to take pictures, but if you ever see the rematch, you're in for a treat!"

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

205

Salandit

Sample Photo

"So you were able to get in on the action! Noivern was cool, too, but Salandit's counterattack was something else!"

How to Complete

  1. Throw fluffruit at the claw marks on the ground to get Salandit to come out. A Noivern will also appear in the distance to the right.

  2. The Noivern will glide down to attack Salandit, and take Salandit’s photo as it retaliates using Poison Gas. Make sure Noivern is in frame.

The Mysterious Heart
Prompt

"This shape is from... Oh, I bet you already know. See if you can get a photo of this Pokémon when it comes by!"

Info

Research Lv. 1 or higher

Target Pokemon

207

Woobat

Sample Photo

"Yep! Brilliant! This is a great photo. Good work!"

How to Complete

  1. In the first room of the ruins, throw five fluffruit into the well hidden on your left near the exit to get five Woobat to fly out (Throw a sixth fluffruit and a Magikarp will appear).

  2. Follow these Woobat then take their photo as they attach themselves to the wall with the heart stamps.

Two Golurk
Prompt

"Ugh, I missed a great photo opportunity! You wanna know what I was trying to photograph? Well...this photo's a hint!"

Info

Research Lv. 2 or higher

Target Pokemon

210

Golurk

Sample Photo

"Yeeeah! This is it! Pretty cool, right? Can I look at this photo just a bit more? Please?"

How to Complete

  1. In the final room of the ruins, throw fluffruit at the stationary Beheeyem upon reaching the ground.

  2. Another stationary Beheeyem will appear later on your left. Hit it with fluffruit as well.

  3. Golurk will begin to fly into the ruins. One of the Golurk will land next to the sleeping Golurk with the Natu on its shoulder. Take a photo of that Golurk as it’s landing.

Groovy Chandelure
Prompt

"I heard Chandelure sometimes breaks out into a happy dance! I wonder what that looks like."

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

211

Chandelure

Sample Photo

"Wow! Look at Chandelure go! Thanks for checking that out for me!"

How to Complete

  1. As you are descending into the final room of the ruins, there will be a Chandelure hiding behind you during your descent. Throw an Illumina Orb at it.

  2. At the end of the level, feed Chandelure a fluffruit. This may be difficult as Chandelure tends to stay in the air most of the time.

  3. Play music after feeding Chandelure, then take a photo of it dancing.

Target Pokemon

212

Umbreon

Sample Photo

"Oh, it was an Umbreon! Thanks for looking into that!"

Myth of the Ruins
Prompt

"Have a look at this crystal here. If I ever get a chance to meet the Pokémon sleeping inside, I'd love to see it grant a wish."

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

213

Jirachi

Sample Photo

"This is an amazing photo! That's the pose Jirachi makes when it's granting a wish."

How to Complete

  1. Activate the six Crystalblooms inside the ruins:

    1. Beside the Meganium statue.

    2. Beside the Wishiwashi statue.

    3. Behind the stalagmites just past the Steelix statue.

    4. Throw Illumina Orbs at the Eldegoss huddled around the Crystalbloom besides the Volcarona statue to get them to leave, then activate that Crystalbloom.

    5. Throw an Illumina Orb at the sleeping Golurk by the Milotic statue to get it to move, then activate the Crystalbloom that was behind the Golurk.

    6. Once all five previous Crystalblooms have been activated, activate the final Crystalbloom near the end of the course in the Crystalbloom Garden.

  2. Jirachi will be sleeping on top of the Steelix statue. Throw an Illumina Orb at it to wake it up.

  3. After activating all of the Crystalblooms, take the alternate path to the glowing altar.

  4. Play music for Jirachi when it reappears.

  5. Jirachi will fly around while the music is playing. Throw Illumina Orbs at Jirachi while it’s flying around.

  6. After successfully hitting Jirachi with an Illumina Orb, immediately play music again once it stops flying around.

  7. Jirachi will start dancing in place, and then begin to pray and cast some sparkles. Take a photo of Jirachi as it’s praying.

Aurus Island Illumina Spot

The Power of Xerneas
Prompt

"The Illumina energy in this area seems particularly strong. We may be able to observe Xerneas behaving in new ways here."

Info

Research Lv. 2

Target Pokemon

214

Xerneas

Sample Photo

"Thank you for investigating! I can tell from this photo there must have been a tremendous amount of energy there!"

How to Complete

  1. In the second room of the level, hit Xerneas’ floating orb with an Illumina Orb.

  2. Xerneas should appear and walk in front of the mural, in between two sleeping Houndoom. Wake up both of the Houndoom by hitting them with fluffruit.

  3. Use fluffruit to lure both of the Houndoom in front of Xerneas.

  4. Once in front of Xerneas, scan the Houndoom to make them howl and face Xerneas.

  5. Take a photo of Xerneas as it uses Geomancy.
