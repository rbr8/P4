Myth of the Jungle
Prompt

"It'd be amazing if you could get a photo of Mew eating a fluffruit."

Info

(Night) Research Lv. 2 + (Day) Research Lv. 2 or higher

Target Pokemon

060

Mew

Sample Photos

"You really are incredible for managing to photograph this once-in-a-lifetime sight!"

How to Complete
  • To get Mew to come out during the day, you must first get a 4-star shot of it at night.

  1. On Jungle (Night) Lv. 2, at the beginning of the level, immediately turn around and play music.

  2. A floating pink orb will appear, and you must successfully hit it with a fluffruit at multiple locations:

    1. As soon as it appears at the start of the level.

    2. Shortly after passing Ariados' web.

    3. On the alternate path through the swamp.

  3. Play music once you reach the ruins to cause Mew to appear. Restart the level and repeat the previous steps if it fails to show up.

  4. Once Mew has appears, face forwards towards the Liepard by the waterfall. Mew will appear directly in front of you for its 4-star shot. Once this has been completed, Mew will always appear in Jungle (Day) Research Levels 2 and 3.

  5. During the day, successfully hit Mew with Illumina Orbs at multiple locations:

    1. At the beginning of the level.

    2. Near the sleeping Venusaur (Spawns behind you, then loops around the swamp before stopping at Venusaur).

    3. By the big tree above the ruins.

    4. In the open area by the waterfall near the end of the level, or above the water if you take the alternate path behind the waterfall.

    5. In the open area by the waterfall near the end of the level, or above the water if you take the alternate path behind the waterfall.

  6. If done correctly, Mew will come down to the ground where it can be fed fluffruit. Take a photo of it as it eats, but be careful not to accidentally hit Mew with fluffruit as it will teleport away.
