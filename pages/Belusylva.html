Myth of the Jungle
Prompt

"It'd be amazing if you could get a photo of Mew eating a fluffruit."

Info

Research Lv. 2 or higher

Target Pokemon

060

Mew

How to Complete

  • To get Mew to come out during the day, you must first get a 4-star shot of it at night.

  • On Jungle (Night) Lv. 2, at the beginning of the level, immediately turn around and play music.

    • As soon as it appears at the start of the level.

    • Shortly after passing Ariados' web.

    • On the alternate path through the swamp.

  • Play music once you reach the ruins for Mew to appear. Restart the level if it fails to show up.

  • Once Mew has appeared, face forwards towards the Liepard by the waterfall. Mew will appear directly in front of you for its 4-star shot.

  • Once this has been completed, Mew will always appear during Jungle (Day) Research Lvs. 2 and 3.

  • During the day, successfully hit Mew with Illumina Orbs at multiple locations:

    • At the beginning of the level

    • Near the sleeping Venusaur

    • By the big tree above the ruins

    • In the open area by the waterfall near the end of the level.

  • If done correctly, Mew will come down to the ground where it can be fed fluffruit. Take a photo of it as it eats, but be careful not to accidentally hit Mew with fluffruit as it will teleport away.

Sample Photos

"You really are incredible for managing to photograph this once-in-a-lifetime sight!"

Little Lost Deerling
Prompt

"This Sawsbuck seemed to be searching for something the whole time I was out doing research. Maybe it got separated from its Deerling..."

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

065

Deerling

How to Complete

  • In the second foggy area, there will be multiple Crystalblooms to your right that span almost the entire length of the area.

  • Light up the first Crystalbloom so that a Deerling comes up to it, then do the same with the second Crystalbloom.

  • Next there will be two Crystalblooms close together. Ignore the one in the front and only light up the Crystalbloom in the back and the Deerling will follow.

  • Afterwards comes the final Crystalbloom. Light up it and Espeon will come to it followed by the Deerling from before. Then a Sawsbuck will come to the Crystalbloom.

  • Take a photo of Deerling as the Pokemon gathered around the Crystalbloom celebrate (This also permanently unlocks the alternate path to the Sunny Spot).

"They found each other! Thank goodness! Did you bring them back together? That's awesome! YOU'RE awesome!"

A Seasonal Gathering
Prompt

"There's something mysterious about this place, don't you think? I get this feeling that the impossible could happen here."

Info

Research Lv. 3

Target Pokemon

066

Sawsbuck

How to Complete

  • Take the alternate path to the Sunny Spot and feed Espeon a fluffruit using the method described in A Calming Air.

  • After eating, Espeon will jump onto the hill on your right. Throw Illumina Orbs at it.

  • In the clearing to your right, two of the four seasonal forms of Deerling and Sawsbuck will be present (which ones present will depend on which seasonal area you went through earlier in the level). Espeon will escort one of the remaining seasonal forms of Deerling and Sawsbuck into the clearing. Throw an Illumina Orb at the Crystalbloom in the middle to get the last remaining seasonal form of Deerling and Sawsbuck to appear in the clearing.

  • Lure all four Sawsbuck close together using fluffruit, scan to get them to all to face you, then play music to get them to howl.

  • Take a photo of all four seasonal forms of Sawsbuck howling.

Sample Photo

"What a photo! I doubt anyone could ever re-create it!"
