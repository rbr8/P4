  1. In the last section of the level, use fluffruit to lure the Pikachu on the beach towards the Stunfisk on the sand bank across from the beach.

Hiding in the Sand
Prompt

"The way Octillery is eyeing the sand over there... Something's about to happen--I just know it!"

Info

Research Lv. 1 or higher

Target Pokemon

090

Stunfisk

Sample Photo

"My hunch about that sand was spot on! I do feel a little bad for Octillery, though."

How to Complete

  1. Take the alternate path to the hidden beach.

  2. To your right there will be an Octillery in the water. Scan the area in front of it to reveal a Stunfisk hiding in the sand and cause it to come out.

  3. Throw fluffruit above the Stunfisk. If lined up correctly, the fluffruit will sink and hit the Stunfisk, angering it and causing it to discharge.

  4. Afterwards, Stunfisk will release a second larger discharge that hits Octillery. Take a photo of Stunfisk as it’s electrocuting Octillery.

Prompt

"Heh heh! Pretty good shot, huh? I saw even more Inkay out there, too, so I bet I could get a group shot of 'em."

Research Lv. 1 or higher

Target Pokemon

087

Inkay

Sample Photo

"Oh! Nice shot! Hey, wait a sec... Aw, man! Your photo's got more Inkay in it than mine!"

How to Complete

  • To complete this request, you need to feed fluffruit to multiple Inkay that appear throughout the level.

  1. The first two Inkay are to your right at the end of the first stretch level before passing under the arch.

  2. After passing under the arch, feed the Inkay to your right as well as the two Inkay from before that have followed you.

  3. On the main path, another Inkay can be found just before passing over the rock.

  4. One Inkay can be found to your left immediately after passing over the rock.

  5. The last three Inkay appear to your right near Seviper.

  6. Towards the end of the course, all of the Inkay that have been fed throughout the level have gathered in one spot. Play music for them.

  7. Take a photo of the group of Inkay as they jump into the air.

  1. Take the alternate path to the seafloor.

  2. Upon reaching the ruins area, a group of Clawitzer can be seen zooming in the distance. One of them will peel off and approach the nearby sleeping Golisopod and blast it with water. Throw an Illumina Orb at this Clawitzer before it shoots so that the blast of water wakes up Golisopod.

  3. When Golisopod wakes up, it will walk over to another nearby Golisopod and the two will begin sparring. Take a photo of the two Golisopod while they are sparring.

Lugia

Sample Photo

How to Complete

  1. Take the alternate path to the seafloor and then unlock access to the second alternate path leading to Lugia’s Den with the method described in A Light in the Depths.

  2. Upon entering Lugia’s Den, Lugia can be seen sleeping to your right. Wake up Lugia by activating both of the Crystalblooms near it:

    1. One is directly next to Lugia and easy to see.

    2. The other is up on a ledge behind Lugia, and a bit harder to see.

  3. Lugia will swim away, but then reappear near the ruins swimming towards you. Successfully hit Lugia with two fluffruits so that it twirls upon passing you. Restart the level and try again if Lugia does not twirl.

  4. Lugia will reappear again, this time above the ruins. Take its photo as it lets out a mighty roar.
