New Pokemon Snap Missions Guide

New Pokemon Snap on the Nintendo Switch is a sequel to the Nintendo 64 Pokemon Snap game. In the game, you explore the Lental Region with over 200 Pokemon available to interact with and take pictures of. New Pokemon Snap also has special missions called LenTalk Requests, which you can complete in the game for extra rewards. They also give you hints about different actions and poses you can find in the levels, so they're well worth checking out for ideas on what to discover. This guide will list all the available LenTalk Requests and how to complete them.
